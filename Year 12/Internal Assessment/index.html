Welcome to our website!

Welcome to our website where we are going to help you manage your homework!

Welcome dear customer. This website was made by Mario Jovanovic for his Computer Science IA. If you would like us to help you we can get you started by creating an account with our platform. You will see in the navigation menu button that has login written on it. You are going to press that button and then you are going to be taken to the menu where you can login to your account or create a new account. If you have already created your account you can Login to your account, but if this is your first time on our website I will ask you to press Create an Account button.

Now when the create an account menu pops up you are going to input your email and password twice and click create an account. After this you are going to go back to the login menu and then click Login this way you can enter your email and then you are going to be able to start managing your homework.
